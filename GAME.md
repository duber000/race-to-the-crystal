# Information about the game

## Components

grid board•each

players has a group of 20 dice tokens that represent their life and move

around the board•4 players per game•2 players can team up•goal is to

capture a power crystal in the middle of the board•players start from

each corner of the board•power crystal is empowered by 4 generators in

each quadrant of the board•players win by holding the power crystal for 3

turns with 12 tokens•each generator is "taken out" by 2 tokens holding

it for 2 turns•each generator "taken out" reduces the players needing to

hold the crystal by 2•tokens have these numbers on their dice

representing their health, 10, 8, 6, 4•6 and 4 tokens can move two

spaces, everyone else moves 2•each token attacks for 1/2 of their value

(10 for 5, 8 for 4 etc) - attacker takes no damange•in random spots

there are mystery squares (equal in each quadrant) that make you flip a

coin - heads you heal to full, tails you head back to start
